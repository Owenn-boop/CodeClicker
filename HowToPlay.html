Code Clicker Instructions

Code Clicker is a simple yet addictive game where your goal is to collect many lines of code as possible. Here's how to play:

1. Click the Button

Click the button to earn one line of code per click. This is the most basic way to earn LOC in the game.

2. Buy Upgrades

Use your earned LOCs to buy upgrades that will increase the number of LOCs you earn per click or per second. Upgrades can be found on the right side of the screen. Each upgrade has a cost and a number of upgrades that can be purchased at a time. The cost of the upgrade will increase each time you purchase it.

3. Maximize Your Earnings

Try to maximize your earnings by purchasing upgrades that give you the most LOCs per second. You can also purchase upgrades that increase the number of upgrades you can purchase at a time. This will allow you to quickly purchase multiple upgrades and increase your earnings even further.

4. Reset Data

If you want to start over, you can reset your data by clicking the "Reset Data" button. This will reset your LOC count and all purchased upgrades. Be careful, as this action cannot be undone.

5. Have Fun!

Code Clicker is a game that is meant to be enjoyed. Don't stress too much about maximizing your earnings or getting the most LOCs. Just have fun and enjoy the game!
